# MeetingBot
Meeting Bot is a technology to detect the faces of the participants invited to the meeting. 

In this case, the meeting is divided into 2 rooms and the participants have been divided into certain rooms (Can be looked in the dataset). Meeting Bot is responsible for ensuring that participants attend the designated room.
